# FintechCheck — Real-Time DeFi Risk Intelligence

**FintechCheck is a real-time systemic risk engine for DeFi.**  
It monitors peg stability, liquidation pressure, and capital flows to detect early signs of market stress before failures happen.

---

## 🚨 The Problem
DeFi failures don’t happen instantly—they build:
- Stablecoins drift off peg  
- Liquidations start clustering  
- Liquidity quietly exits  

Most tools track these individually. None combine them into a unified risk signal.

---

## ⚡ The Solution
FintechCheck introduces a **Composite Risk Engine (CRE)**:
- **Peg Stress** → detects depeg risk early  
- **Liquidation Stress** → monitors cascading positions  
- **Flow Pressure** → tracks capital movement  

These signals combine into a **single real-time risk score**.
